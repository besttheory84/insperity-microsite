About Insperity
Insperity, a trusted advisor to America's best businesses since 1986, provides human resources and business solutions designed to help improve business performance. Offering the most comprehensive suite of products and services available in the marketplace, Insperity delivers administrative relief, better benefits, reduced liabilities and a systematic way to improve productivity.
Insperity has been a proud supporter of local chambers for years. Working with ACCE, Insperity has funded projects including:
  • 2007 and 2012 Schapiro studies
  • A Guide to Intercity Visits (2008 publication)
  • An Examination of Chamber Revenue Models
  • ACCE University sponsorship and content support
  • ACCE.org HR content and resources
