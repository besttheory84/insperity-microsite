What is the Insperity affinity program?
Together, our organizations provide local chambers and their members preferred access to industry-leading HR and business performance solutions.
The Insperity® affinity program offers an opportunity for chamber partners to create a revenue stream through member engagement. It's an association where everyone benefits.
Through this partnership, the essential role of the chamber in local markets is reinforced, member retention efforts are strengthened, and new revenue streams are discovered, supporting the long-term prosperity of the chamber and its members.
What chambers receive
Free payroll services through member incentive plan
Preferred pricing on select Insperity HR solutions
New revenue source through member engagement
Access to member-centric marketing and program management
What members receive
Preferred pricing on select Insperity HR solutions
Added value to their existing chamber membership
A local, community-based business performance advisor

How it works
Chamber members will receive a discount on Insperity products and services. The chamber of commerce will receive a percentage of the revenue for each member it refers who becomes an Insperity client.
With an average annual spend of only $5,000 per member-customer, your chamber could earn $20,000 from just 50 members who become Insperity clients.
$5,000 x 50 = $250,000 x 8% = $20,000
